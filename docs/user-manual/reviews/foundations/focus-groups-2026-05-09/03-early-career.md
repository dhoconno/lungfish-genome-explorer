# Foundations focus group 3: early-career scientists

**Date:** 2026-05-09
**Method:** Five early-career persona (less than 2 years post-degree) reading the eight foundations chapters as a unit. See `synthesis.md` for cross-cutting issues.

## Key findings from this group

- Wastewater is repeatedly punted with no forward link. Three of five early-career personas have wastewater workflows.
- Ct (cycle threshold) is undefined despite multiple references.
- Multi-user and multi-sample workflows are absent; the project model is single-sample only.
- Provenance is the standout chapter; clinical and surveillance personas would lift it directly into validation packages.
- "So what should you do with this?" refrain divides reviewers; three of five find it patronizing.

## What worked

- Phred score table and FILTER flag table both got lifted to bench notebooks.
- Diploid-vs-haploid panel in chapter 5 was the single most-extracted artifact.
- Concrete fixture-tied numbers (86,281 read pairs, etc.) ground every chapter.
